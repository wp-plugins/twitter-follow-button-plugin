== Description ==
With this plugin, you can embed Twitter Follow Button to let your visitor follow you instantly by just clicking on the button. Add the Follow Button to your website to increase engagement and create a lasting connection with your audience.

== Installation ==

* Download "twitter-follow-button-plugin.zip" to your computer.
* Login as administrator to your Wordpress website
* Click on Plugins --> Add New
* On "Install Plugins" page, clik on "Upload" 
* Browse and select "twitter-follow-button-plugin.zip" and click on "Install Now"
* You can activate it now
* Finally, to adjust its setting, click on "Twitter Follow Button" on the left side of menu
* Visit http://cmsvoteup.com for updated news for this plugin
